About Stellar Blockchain

The Future of Asset Trading

Stellar is a decentralized, open-source blockchain network that enables fast, cross-border transactions between any pair of currencies. With transaction times of just 3-5 seconds and fees of less than $0.01, Stellar is revolutionizing financial systems worldwide.

The Stellar network supports the trading of all forms of value - currencies, securities, and more - with transactions that settle in seconds. The network is designed so all the world's financial systems can work together on a single platform.

Stellar's native asset, Lumens (XLM), serves as a bridge between currencies and helps reduce transaction costs. It's the perfect digital asset for cross-border payments and asset trading.
